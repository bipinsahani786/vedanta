## **VEDANTA PLACEMENT AGENCY** 

## **Recruitment Management ERP & CRM Platform** 

## **Software Requirement Specification (SRS)** 

## **Project Objective** 

Develop a fully automated Recruitment Management ERP & CRM Platform for Vedanta Placement Agency that manages candidate registration, renewals, agreements, payments, service charges, application tracking, CRM operations, analytics, and recruitment workflows with minimum manual intervention. 

The system must provide a professional experience for Candidates, Schools, HR Team, and Administrators. 

## **MODULE 1: CANDIDATE REGISTRATION SYSTEM** 

## **Registration Flow** 

When a candidate visits the registration page, the system must display: 

- Terms & Conditions 

- Registration Agreement 

- Refund Policy 

- Service Rules 

- Registration Validity Rules 

Before making any payment, the candidate must: 

1. Complete Registration Form 

2. Add Digital Signature 

3. Accept Terms & Conditions 

Until agreement acceptance: 

- Registration Plans remain locked 

- Payment Button remains disabled 

After acceptance: 

- Registration Plans unlock automatically • Payment can be initiated

## **MODULE 4: APPLICATION VALIDITY SYSTEM** 

Each registration provides: 

Maximum 3 Interview / Job Opportunities 

System should automatically track: 

- Schools Applied 

- Interviews Scheduled 

- Interviews Attended 

- Selection Status 

- Remaining Opportunities 

Validity remains active until: 

1. Three Opportunities are Used OR 

2. Candidate Gets Selected 

No time-based expiry is required.

## **MODULE 5: REGISTRATION EXPIRY & RENEWAL** 

## **Registration Expiry** 

When all 3 opportunities are consumed: 

Status: Registration Expired 

Dashboard Action: Renew Registration 

## **Successful Placement** 

When candidate gets selected: 

Status: Successfully Placed 

Registration Status: Closed 

Remaining opportunities become invalid automatically. 

## **Renewal System** 

Dashboard must display: 

Renew Registration Button 

Candidate can: 

- View Previous Plan 

- View Payment History 

- Select Renewal Plan 

- Make Payment 

After successful payment: 

- Registration Reactivated 

- Application Count Reset 

- Existing Profile Retained
